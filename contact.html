Common Questions

Quick answers before your visit.

Walk-ins are welcome for basic consultations, but we recommend booking in advance to avoid waiting, especially for specialist treatments like implants or braces consultations. You can call, WhatsApp, or use the form above.

Please bring a valid ID and any previous dental X-rays or records if you have them. For children, a parent or guardian must accompany them. Arrive 5–10 minutes early to complete any initial paperwork.

We accept most major dental insurance plans and can help you understand your coverage. Please call us in advance with your insurance details so we can confirm coverage before your appointment.

We accept cash, all major debit/credit cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and net banking. For major procedures like implants, we offer flexible EMI options to make treatment affordable.

Yes, there is parking available in the Sushant Lok 2 area. Block G has space for two-wheelers and four-wheelers nearby. If you have trouble finding parking, call us and we'll guide you.
